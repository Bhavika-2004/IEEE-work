About Us

IEEE CIET Student Branch has been actively working together to design, create and produce work that we are proud of to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. In addition to it, we also administer various events at the university level as well as under the Delhi Section.

WIE
At Chitkara University

IEEE CIET Student Branch has the WIE affinity council functioning under its umbrella and is currently chaired by Srishti Pal. The group has been exuberantly working to come up with events that will contribute to the uprising of women in the technical world. For this, we have organized numerous expert talks and competitions at the university level as well as the regional level to involve maximum female participants.
